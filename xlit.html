DIN 31635 Transliteration

The DIN 31635 transliteration is a phonetic representation based on the German standard DIN 31635:1982.
It shows only the sounds that are actually recited/pronounced during Quranic recitation,
making it easier to read and understand for those learning Arabic pronunciation.
