p. 1783

Sūra CIII.

’Aṣr, or Time through the Ages.

In the name of God, Most Gracious,
Most Merciful.

1. By (the Token of)
Time (through the Ages),

2. Verily Man
Is in loss,

3. Except such as have Faith,
And do righteous deeds,
And (join together)
In the mutual teaching
Of Truth, and of
Patience and Constancy.
