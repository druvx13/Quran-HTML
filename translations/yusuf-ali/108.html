p. 1798

Sūra CVIII.

Kauthar, or Abundance.

In the name of God, Most Gracious,
Most Merciful.

1. To thee have We
Granted the Fount
(of Abundance).

2. Therefore to thy Lord
Turn in Prayer
And Sacrifice.

3. For he who hateth thee,—
He will be cut off
(From Future Hope).
