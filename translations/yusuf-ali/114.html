Sūra CXIV.

Nās, or Mankind.

In the name of God, Most Gracious,
Most Merciful.

1. Say: I seek refuge
With the Lord
And Cherisher of Mankind,

2. The King (or Ruler)
Of Mankind,

3. The God (or Judge)
Of Mankind,—

4. From the mischief
Of the Whisperer
(Of Evil), who withdraws
(After his whisper),—

5. (The same) who whispers
Into the hearts of Mankind,—

6. Among Jinns
And among Men.
