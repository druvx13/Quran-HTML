### About the Translation

The Pickthall translation is one of the most respected English translations of the Qur'an. M.M. Pickthall (1875-1936) was an English Islamic scholar and writer who converted to Islam. His translation, published in 1938, is known for its literary quality and faithfulness to the original Arabic text.
