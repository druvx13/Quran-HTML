### About the Translation

J.M. Rodwell (1808-1900) was an English clergyman and orientalist. His translation, first published in 1861 and revised in 1876, was one of the first to arrange the Suras in chronological order rather than the traditional order found in Arabic manuscripts. This chronological arrangement helps readers follow the development of Muhammad's thought and the progressive revelation of Islamic doctrine. The 1876 edition includes an insightful introduction by G. Margoliouth, M.A., providing valuable historical and literary context.
